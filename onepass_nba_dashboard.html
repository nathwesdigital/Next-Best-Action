Decision Map

The deck points to rapid early activation: Flybuys, app install and first division link are heavily front-loaded. The next best action should then shift to the next linked division.

0

Start with the required links

Flybuys and app install both show strong same-day behaviour. Make these the first onboarding checks after trial or paid start.

24h

Close the fastest gaps

Within 24 hours, 72% of Flybuys linkers and 66% of app installers have already acted after join.

2d

Ask for the second division

The median second linked division happens around day 2 after trial or paid start.

4-9d

Build breadth

Third, fourth, fifth and sixth division links cluster around days 3.9, 5.9, 9.1 and 9.2.
